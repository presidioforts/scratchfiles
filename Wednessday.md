
**Opening:**

*"Thank you for joining today. I’m going to walk you through the critical steps of implementing a smooth and successful VM migration process. Each step builds a reliable, automated system that reduces risk and improves efficiency. Our approach makes the process faster, more reliable, and fully automated."*

---

**Step 1: Validate VM Migration Under Controlled Conditions**

*"The first step is validating VM migrations under controlled conditions. This involves running functional tests to ensure that the migration process is safe and reliable. This gives us a solid foundation, helping us understand how the migration automation will behave in production and prepare for any potential issues."*

---

**Step 2: Develop Migration Criteria**

*"Next, we create a checklist of qualification criteria. This checklist helps us determine whether a VM can safely migrate or if it needs to be rebuilt. Each VM is evaluated based on specific requirements, ensuring the migration process is handled correctly. Standardizing this decision-making process helps avoid risks."*

---

**Step 3: Develop a Scripted Migration Process**

*"The third step is to develop a detailed, scripted process for VM migrations. This script is the backbone of our automation, ensuring that each migration is consistent and repeatable. With this standardized procedure, we reduce errors and improve the overall reliability of the migration process."*

---

**Step 4: Create an Automated Orchestration Layer**

*"At this stage, we combine the checklist and the scripted process to create an automated orchestration layer. This enables teams to manage and initiate migrations independently with minimal manual intervention, making the process scalable across the enterprise."*

---

**Step 5: Integrate with ServiceNow**

*"Next, we integrate the orchestration layer into ServiceNow as a catalog request item. This links the migration process with ServiceNow’s data management, ensuring all requests are tracked, managed, and executed seamlessly."*

---

**Step 6: Link to Change Management**

*"We then connect the orchestration layer to ServiceNow change requests. This ensures that all migrations follow change management policies, reducing the risk of unauthorized changes and enhancing control and compliance."*

---

**Step 7: Conduct Comprehensive Testing**

*"Finally, we perform comprehensive testing. This step validates the entire migration process to ensure the solution is robust and ready for enterprise-wide deployment. Testing helps us identify and address any remaining issues, ensuring the migration process is as reliable as possible."*

---

**Conclusion:**

*"By following these steps, we establish a structured, efficient, and reliable approach to VM migration. This process aligns with our organizational goals and operational requirements, delivering a streamlined and scalable solution for managing data center migrations."*
