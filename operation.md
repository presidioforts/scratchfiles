4. Establishing SMEeagle India for 24/7 Global Support

Formation of SMEeagle India: In collaboration with EGS (India) Breakfix CI and CD product stream managers, I successfully spearheaded the formation of the SMEeagle India team, consisting of 4 skilled engineers. This team operates alongside the US-based SMEeagle team, providing seamless support to developers and the India-based development and product teams. The establishment of this team has significantly expanded our support capabilities, fostering cross-regional collaboration.

Ensuring 24/7 Operational Coverage: To maintain continuous global support, we implemented a structured schedule of daily sync-up calls during India business hours, where both US and India teams address escalations. The India-based SMEeagle team participates in these calls during the evening, coinciding with the US morning shift. This coordination ensures a fully operational 24-hour support cycle, enabling us to provide uninterrupted service to developers.

Driving Operational Efficiency: Through effective collaboration between the US and India SMEeagle teams, we have achieved enhanced operational efficiency. This approach not only accelerates issue resolution but also ensures timely support for developers across time zones, allowing us to meet business demands and deliver high-quality support around the clock.
