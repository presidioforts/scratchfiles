CIWAT Platform Transition Team Structure

Overview:

The CIWAT Platform Transition Team, part of the Engineering Enablement Platform Service Group, is responsible for ensuring the smooth operation, performance, and reliability of the CIWAT microservice platform. The team is organized into three specialized units, each focusing on distinct areas: incident management, root cause analysis, and performance monitoring. Together, these teams ensure uninterrupted service, continuous platform improvement, and high performance.


---

1. CIWAT Breakfix and Operation Team

Mission:
This team is responsible for managing production incidents, triaging alerts, and maintaining platform stability. They are the first responders to platform issues, ensuring quick resolution to prevent downtime.

Key Roles and Responsibilities:

Alert Triaging and Incident Management:

Monitor and prioritize alerts in real time, ensuring prompt acknowledgment and resolution.

Coordinate between US and India time zones, ensuring 24/7 coverage for incident response.


Breakfix Support:

Quickly address breakfix incidents to ensure minimal impact on platform availability.

Liaise with engineering teams when issues exceed the operational scope.


Production Environment Management (PCF):

Maintain and ensure the stability of the CIWAT platform across Pivotal Cloud Foundry (PCF) environments, both in production and non-production.


MongoDB Database Management:

Support MongoDB infrastructure to ensure optimal performance and data integrity.


Backup and Knowledge Sharing:

Praveen will train Mary and Bruce to handle alerts during US hours.

Maintain detailed documentation for standard incident handling processes.



Coverage:

Primary Ownership (US Time): Praveen, Mary, Bruce

India Coverage: Vishal

Backup Support: Continuous training ensures seamless coverage across time zones.



---

2. CIWAT Platform Functional SME and RCA Team

Mission:
This team of Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) is responsible for understanding the functional and technical aspects of the CIWAT platform. They handle Root Cause Analysis (RCA) for incidents and defects while providing strategic recommendations for improving platform services and architecture.

Key Roles and Responsibilities:

Root Cause Analysis (RCA):

Investigate incidents to identify root causes, reviewing the codebase, services, databases, and infrastructure.

Document findings and escalate issues to engineering when necessary.


SME Advisory and Functional Expertise:

Act as SMEs, providing detailed insights into the platform’s architecture, services, and functionality.

Offer recommendations for service and performance improvements.


Code and Service Review:

Conduct regular codebase and service reviews, providing feedback to enhance reliability and performance.


Collaboration with Engineering:

Escalate unresolved defects or complex issues to engineering after conducting detailed RCA.

Work with engineering teams to ensure efficient resolution and continuous improvement.


Production Support:

Provide expert support during production incidents, particularly when deeper functional or technical expertise is needed.



Coverage:

Team Members: Shaun, Praveen, Vishal, Akhil, Sarada, Dheeraj, Arun

Escalation Path: Issues unresolved by the RCA team will be escalated to the engineering team.



---

3. CIWAT Performance, Monitoring, and Observability Engineering Team

Mission:
This team ensures the platform’s performance and reliability by monitoring system health, conducting capacity planning, and identifying optimization opportunities. They ensure the platform operates efficiently and meets service-level agreements (SLAs).

Key Roles and Responsibilities:

Reliability and Observability:

Set up and maintain monitoring tools to track system health and performance.

Ensure visibility into the platform’s infrastructure and microservices to quickly identify bottlenecks.


SLA Adherence and Monitoring:

Continuously monitor platform performance against SLAs and ensure system stability.


Capacity Planning and Load Testing:

Regularly conduct capacity planning and stress testing to ensure the platform can handle future loads.


Performance Optimization:

Identify inefficiencies and bottlenecks across infrastructure and services, providing recommendations for optimization.


Incident Performance Support:

Collaborate with other teams during performance-related incidents, providing real-time support to resolve performance issues.



Coverage:

Team Members: Bill, Manda, Dheeraj, Praveen, Sarada, Arun

Key Focus: Continuous performance monitoring and ensuring platform reliability through proactive performance tuning.



---

Key Escalation and Communication Channels

1. Production Incident Escalation:

The Breakfix and Operation Team triages and handles initial incident management.

For incidents requiring deeper analysis, issues are escalated to the CIWAT Platform Functional SME and RCA Team.

If defects or complex issues are identified, they are escalated to engineering for resolution.



2. Performance Issues:

Performance-related incidents are monitored and managed by the CIWAT Performance, Monitoring, and Observability Engineering Team.

Severe performance issues are addressed collaboratively with the Breakfix Team and RCA Team.



3. Collaboration with Engineering:

Engineering handles code-level fixes or complex defects that the RCA and Performance teams cannot resolve. Continuous collaboration ensures efficient resolution and ongoing improvements.





---

Conclusion:

The CIWAT Platform Transition Team structure ensures that all aspects of the platform's operations, performance, and support are covered. With clear roles and responsibilities across the Breakfix and Operation Team, the CIWAT Platform Functional SME and RCA Team, and the Performance Engineering Team, this structure allows for efficient incident management, continuous platform optimization, and high reliability. The collaboration between these teams ensures that all issues are handled promptly and effectively, with strategic oversight and performance management always at the forefront.
