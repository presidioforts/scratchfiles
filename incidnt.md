**Incident Management Process for EPL-X and CIWAT Observability Incidents**

1. **Incident Identification & Ticket Creation**

   - Any observability platform-generated incidents related to EPL-X and CIWAT products must be logged as tickets in the **SDLC DevOps queue**.

2. **Automated Ticket Distribution**

   - **DevOps JIRA automation** will automatically route the ticket to the appropriate **SME queue engineers**, who belong to specialized groups such as the **EPL-X and CIWAT SCP** teams.

3. **Incident Analysis & Resolution Planning**

   - SMEs will **analyze** the ticket, including identifying the root cause, assessing the impact, and determining an immediate resolution plan.
   - Based on their findings, they will create a **product backlog item** (for SCP or EPL-X engineering) to address and resolve the issue.

4. **Production Incident Documentation**

   - All production incidents must be **documented** to ensure visibility for the **platform service team**, including details such as incident timestamp, affected components, impact analysis, root cause summary, and resolution steps.
   - The goal is to keep the team informed of ongoing issues and resolutions.
